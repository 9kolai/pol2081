Group work 1:

Extract hypotheses from editorials

  1. Identify independent and dependent variables.
  2. Use this to formulate at least three hypotheses:
    • With different units of analysis
    • Both negative and positive
  3. Write your hypotheses on sticky notes or upload on padlet.com/nikogad/

How can we improve these hypotheses?

  • What is the dependent and the independent variable?
  • What is the unit of analysis?
  • Could any terms be clarified further?
  • Is the hypothesis positively or negatively directed?

Group work 2:

Create research designs to test hypotheses

  1. Choose at least three hypotheses from previous group work
  2. To test each of these hypotheses create:
    • An experiment
    • A small-n multi case study
    • A large-n multi case study
  3. Describe your research designs on sticky notes or upload on padlet.com/nikogad/

How can we asess these research designs?

  • Internal validity?
  • External validity?
  • For small-n case studies: Is this a most similar or a most different case study design?

Key terms

  • Independent and dependent variables
  • Unit of analysis
  • Negative and positive hypotheses
  • Experiments
  • Single case studies
  • Small-n multi case studies
  • Large-n multi case studies
  • Internal validity
  • External validity
  • Most similar and most different case studies
