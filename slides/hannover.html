Using Digital Tools to Re-invigorate Participation in Party Politics: The Case of Alternativet

by Nikolai Gad, PhD candidate, Newcastle University

Digital Democratic Innovations in Political Parties

The Case of Alternativet

  • Danish Political Party est. Nov. 2013
  • Three themes: Sustainable transformation, new political culture, social innovation
  • Political programme to be developed by citizens at open workshops called "Political Laboratories"
  • Experiments with online participation

Methods & Data

  • 19 stakeholders interviewed
  • members of staff in the party, activist volunteers, an MP, a local councillor, party candidates for upcoming local elections, and members holding various positions of trust within the party such as board members in the national organisation as well as in local branches
  • 13 male, 5 female and one interviewee who did not identify as neither male nor female
  • Next step: Membership survey

Models of intra-party democracy

Model Main focus Mechanism Pace Type of part. Key characteristics
Crowdsourcing Output Aggregation of views Quick Individual Direct participation by the many
Developmental Action Empowerment/direct action Collective/individual Coordination and mobilisation of collective action
Deliberative Procedural Development of views Slow Collective Listening and acknowledging different views; consider all sides of argument.
Delegation Procedural Accountability Medium Collective Election of delegates; holding them accountabie

Platform affordances and intra-party democracy

Model Online Platforms Affordances
Crowdsourcing Appgree; Pol.is; AlleOs; Online vote for candidate nominations Aggregative input; one-click actions; up/down voting; support and continuous validation of policies
Developmental AlleOs & Facebook groups Event fucntionalities; group memberships; direct communication channels; templates for new policy proposals and support for finding co-authors.
Deliberative Dialog; Facebook groups; (live streaming of meetings; AlleOs) Posting comments on policies; posting links as evidence; online chat
Delegation AlleOs; live streaming of meetings Q&A with elected representatives
